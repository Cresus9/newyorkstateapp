    • Welcome to Buffalo City

    Buffalo City, known for its rich history and cultural heritage, is the second-largest city in the state of New York. It's renowned for its historical landmarks and vibrant arts scene.

    History

    Buffalo grew rapidly in the 19th and early 20th centuries due to its strategic location on the Great Lakes and the Erie Canal. The city played a significant role in the industrial revolution.

    Top 3 Historical Events in Buffalo:

    1. 1825: Completion of the Erie Canal, connecting Buffalo to NYC.
    2. 1901: Buffalo hosted the Pan-American Exposition.
    3. 1959: Opening of the St. Lawrence Seaway.
