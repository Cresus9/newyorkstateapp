    • Welcome to New York City

    New York City, often simply called New York, is the most populous city in the United States. It is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and Times Square.

    History

    Founded in 1624 as New Amsterdam by the Dutch and later renamed New York after the English took control in 1664. The city's rich history is evident in its neighborhoods, museums, and historic sites.

    Top 3 Historical Events in NYC:

    1. 1624: Establishment by Dutch colonists as New Amsterdam.
    2. 1664: English conquest and renaming to New York.
    3. 1886: Dedication of the Statue of Liberty.
