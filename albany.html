    • Welcome to Albany

    Albany, the capital of New York State, has a rich history dating back to the 17th century.

    History

    Founded by Dutch colonists in 1614, Albany has played a significant role in the history of the United States.

    Top 3 Historical Events in Albany:

    1. 1614: Establishment by Dutch colonists.
    2. 1797: Became the official capital of New York State.
    3. 1807: Erie Canal construction began.
